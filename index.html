HOW TO PLAY

It is the year 2021. You are the founder of coffee up roasters! your job is to fulfill orders from customers by pressing the listed category keys followed by their INGREDIENTS keys as shown on the legend to the right.

As the game goes on, the number of orders per level increases.

You are given 15 seconds per single order, and when you press a wrong key, you will need to restart the entire order. If either your timer or score (sales) falls to or below zero, you lose.

    Key binds
  • P = pause/unpause
  • zero = quit when paused
  • Space = starts game/restart when game is over
  • M = Play/Pause Audio
  • N = to play/pause one of my beats! no yoink pls
